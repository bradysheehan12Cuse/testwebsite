Frequently Asked Questions

How much does junk removal cost in Worcester County?
Our pricing depends on the volume and type of items being removed. We offer free estimates and competitive rates. Most single-item pickups start around $75, while full truckloads vary based on the amount of junk.
What items can you NOT remove?
We cannot remove hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, asbestos, or medical waste. We also don't handle items that require special disposal permits. Contact us if you're unsure about specific items.
Do you offer same-day junk removal service?
Yes! We offer same-day service when possible and emergency service for urgent situations. Call 508-366-7642 to check our availability for today.
How do I prepare for junk removal?
Just point out what you want removed! We handle all the lifting, loading, and cleanup. Make sure we have clear access to the items and that you've removed any personal belongings you want to keep.
